Welcome to the Groups Prototype!

For the sake of this demo let's pretend that you clicked on a hypothes.is link and have been taken to this page.

You are now seeeing and making annotations under the IntrepidArmadillo group.

There are a couple of tasks we will cover and if you experience difficulties or confussion with any of them please email JakeHartnell@hypothes.is.

Please complete the following:

  1. Create an account. You can get your confirmation email here, as this is a prototype this is not a real hypothes.is account no real confirmation email will be sent.
  2. Make an annotation under the IntrepidArmadillo group (select some text and choose the ) icon.
  3. Make a private annotation that is visible only to you.
  4. Share the link to group.
  5. Reply to annotation that is not in the group.
  6. Create a new group! Give it whatever name you choose.
